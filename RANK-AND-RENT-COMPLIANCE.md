# Rank and Rent Compliance Rules

## Content Guidelines

### Never Claim Past Projects or Specific Clients
- Do not reference specific past projects or client names
- Frame scenarios as examples of the type of engagement we can handle
- Invite contact to share real references upon request

### No Fabricated Information
- Do not fabricate timelines or outcomes
- Do not make unverifiable claims
- Do not create testimonials, review stars, or badges without sources

### Tax and Legal Disclaimers
- 1031 defers income tax on qualifying real property and does not remove transfer or documentary taxes
- Educational content only, not tax, legal, or investment advice
- If DST or TIC is mentioned, include a securities disclaimer and provide introductions only

## Example Capability Format

All example capabilities must include:
- Disclaimer: "Example of the type of engagement we can handle"
- Service type or location
- Scope description
- Client situation (generic, not specific)
- Our approach (general methodology)
- Expected outcome (general, not specific)
- Contact CTA with location reference
